About Me

Hi, I’m the creator of Green Star — a digital space built with passion, purpose, and creativity.

I’m a web developer, designer, video editor, and programming enthusiast. My journey began with a simple curiosity about how websites work and how visuals shape our online experience. Over time, that curiosity grew into a full-blown passion for building meaningful, modern, and interactive digital content.

With every line of code I write, every video I edit, and every design I create, I aim to tell a story — your story, my story, or one the world needs to hear. Green Star reflects this spirit — a brand focused on visual storytelling, technology, and creative freedom.

Here, you’ll find content about web design, development tips, video editing tricks, creative projects, and personal thoughts on learning and growth in the digital space. Whether you're a fellow creator, a curious learner, or someone looking to connect, I’m happy you're here.

Let’s grow, create, and inspire — together.
